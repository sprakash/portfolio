Priya's Shakti

An augmented reality comic book that fights gender-based violence.

My journey with Priya's Shakti has been that of a voice artist, translator for the comic
from english to hindi and now co-producer.



SHORT FILM

As a voice artist I gave voice to what was a short film in the beginning, Parvati Saves the World.

Featured on VICE's Creator's Project see here


A new re-mix film using dozens of Bollywood Hindu mythological films from the 1970s, similar to DJ Spooky’s remix of “Birth of a Nation” but this focuses on gender-based violence. In three acts. priyashakti.com Watch Act 2, Watch Act 3

COMIC BOOK

I joined Ram Devineni, the creator and upon exploring the imagery of the film, we decided to create a comic book called, Priya's Shakti.


Dan Goldman came on board as the comic book artist.

AUGMENTED REALITY

Blippar, was building A/R apps so we decided to test the comic book with some A/R elements. Priya's shakti comic books and street murals both have A/R elements that can be accessed via Blippar app.

There are several murals of Priya around the world
